Facts and Cool Things About Vietnam

Quick Summary

If you didn't know already, Vietnam is a country in Southeast Asia. Having been born there and recently visited over there, I thought talking about some popular sites would be interesting. Vietnam can be recongized by it's extreme hot weather, cultural food, and tourist locations.

Can Thơ is one of the major cities of Vietnam can be easily recongized by the following:

  • Long rivers
  • Market/Restaurant boats
  • Fish market like docks
  • Hot and humid temperatures
  • Farm lands

Ninh Kieu Wharf is a highly visited tourist spot (mostly at night). As seen in the image, this site uses the river of Can Tho and is more modernized. Visiting this area you can expect:

  • Large boat cruise
  • A large scenic bridge
  • Cooler breezes
  • Several large parks
  • Several high end restaurants
